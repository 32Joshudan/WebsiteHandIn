Sonic the Hedgehog 2 is a 2022 action-adventure comedy film based on the video game series published by Sega, and the sequel to Sonic the Hedgehog (2020). Directed by Jeff Fowler and written by Pat Casey, Josh Miller, and John Whittington, it stars Ben Schwartz reprising his role as the voice of Sonic the Hedgehog, alongside Colleen O'Shaughnessey, Idris Elba, Jim Carrey, James Marsden, Tika Sumpter, Lee Majdoub, Adam Pally, Tom Butler, Shemar Moore and Natasha Rothwell. The film follows Sonic and Tails, who join forces to find the Master Emerald before Doctor Robotnik and Knuckles the Echidna can.

The Super Mario Bros. Movie is an upcoming computer-animated film based on Nintendo's Mario video game franchise, with Chris Pratt as the voice of Mario.

The film is produced by Universal Pictures and Illumination, in association with Nintendo, and distributed by Universal. It is the third feature film adaptation of the Mario franchise, following the Japanese anime film Super Mario Bros.: The Great Mission to Rescue Princess Peach! (1986) and the American live-action film Super Mario Bros. (1993).

The film is directed by Aaron Horvath and Michael Jelenic from a screenplay by Matthew Fogel. The film's ensemble voice cast also includes Anya Taylor-Joy, Charlie Day, Jack Black, Keegan-Michael Key, Seth Rogen, Fred Armisen, Sebastian Maniscalco, Charles Martinet, and Kevin Michael Richardson.
